# Puzzle 36 — Romanian

Source: UKLO 2018 — https://www.uklo.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/2018_1-Romanian.pdf

## Context

Romanian is spoken by about 26 million people in Romania and Moldova, and is descended from Latin. The puzzle explores how Romanian expresses 'a' and 'the'. Note that 'a' and 'a-breve' are pronounced differently and the difference matters.
