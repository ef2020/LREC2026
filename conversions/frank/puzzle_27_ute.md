# Puzzle 27 — Ute

Source: UKLO 2020 — https://www.uklo.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/2020_R2_4-Ute.pdf

## Context

Ute is spoken by 1640 people in an area extending from California to Colorado in the United States. It is part of the Uto-Aztecan language family. Here are some Ute sentences and their translations in English.

1. wuukaqhamitumu 'umu 'aapachi 'uway navutigikyaqha — The workers imitated the boy.
2. 'aapachiu 'umu po'oqway — The boys are writing.
3. sivaatuchiu 'umu sarichi 'uway taaqha — The goat kicked the dog.
4. mamachi 'u sivaatuchiu 'umu tunavovoqwa — The woman hunted the goats.
5. 'uamitu 'u kaay — The farmer is singing.
6. tuachi 'u sivaatuchiu 'umu paqhaqhaqa — The child killed the goats.
7. po'omitu 'u 'uamitu 'uway navutigi — The writer is imitating the farmer.
8. wuukamitu 'u 'ivi — The worker is drinking.
9. mamachiu 'umu wuukamitu 'uway po'otikyaqha — The women taught the worker.
10. sarichi 'u tuachiu 'umu taay — The dog is kicking the children.
11. 'uaqhamitumu 'umu sivaatuchi 'uway tunavovoqwaqha — The farmers hunted the goat.
12. po'oqwamitumu 'umu kaaqhay — The writers are singing.
