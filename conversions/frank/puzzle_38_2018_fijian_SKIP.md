# Puzzle 38 — Fijian [SKIP]

Source: UKLO 2018 — https://www.uklo.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/2018_4-Fijian.pdf

## Reason for skipping

This puzzle cannot be converted to a standard Match-Up format because the translation questions require understanding which possessive classifier to use (me- for drinkables, ke- for edibles/patient, no- for general possession, or no classifier for inalienable possession). The English translations include parenthetical explanations (e.g., "his club (that he owns)", "her oyster (that she'll sell)") that provide the possessive context needed to determine the correct Fijian form. Without these explanations, the Match-Up would be ambiguous; with them, the answers are given away. The presence of the explanatory words in the translations prevents the application of the standard conversion procedure.
