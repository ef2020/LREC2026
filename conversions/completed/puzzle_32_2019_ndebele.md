# Puzzle 32 — Ndebele

Source: UKLO 2019 — https://www.uklo.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/2019_9-Ndebele.pdf

## Context

Ndebele is spoken in parts of southern Africa (mainly Zimbabwe). Here are some phrases from the Ndebele language along with their English translations.
