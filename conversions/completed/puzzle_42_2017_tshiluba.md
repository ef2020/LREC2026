# Puzzle 42 — Tshiluba

Source: UKLO 2017 — https://www.uklo.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/2017_4.-Tshiluba.pdf

## Context

Tshiluba, spoken by about 6 million people, is one of the official languages of the Democratic Republic of the Congo. It belongs to the Bantu language group. In Bantu languages, prefixes distinguish singular and plural nouns, and are repeated on related words including verbs.

Additional given: balunda = friends; cimuma = fruit; bimuma = fruits.

## Notes
- Context table (12 sentence pairs) and Match-Up table (8 fill-in-the-blank items) combined into a single Match-Up table of 20 items.
