## Context

Bulgarian noun phrases in the form 'quantifier' (numeral or "many") + noun:



Note: Bulgarian normally uses the Cyrillic alphabet, but this problem uses the Roman alphabet instead, with c for a "ts" sound and ə for the sound at the end of "sofa".
