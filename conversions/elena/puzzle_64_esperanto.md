# Puzzle 64 — Esperanto

Source: UKLO 2012 — https://www.uklo.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/2012.5-Esperanto.pdf

## Context

Esperanto is an artificial language created by Ludwig Lazarus Zamenhof in 1887 for international communication. Estimates of active speakers range from 100,000 to 2,000,000. The letters 'ĝ' and 'ĉ' are pronounced like 'g' and 'ch' in 'gem' and 'chase'.

| Esperanto | English |

| La hundo ĉasis la katon. | The dog chased the cat. |
