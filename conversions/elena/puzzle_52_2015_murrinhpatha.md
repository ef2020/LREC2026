# Puzzle 52 — Murrinhpatha

Source: UKLO 2015 — https://www.uklo.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/2015_7-Murrinhpatha.pdf

## Context

Murrinhpatha is a language spoken by about 3,000 people in Australia's Northern Territory. It uses sounds not found in English (rr, nh, th). Abbreviations: [2s] = two people who are siblings, [2n] = two people who are not siblings, [1] = singular.

Additional info: -thurr = to tread on something; mamkamit = I obstructed it (by hand); mamkurrk = I scratched it (by hand).
