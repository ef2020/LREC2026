# Puzzle 51 — Karelian

Source: UKLO 2015 — https://www.uklo.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/2015_1-Karelian.pdf

## Context

Karelian is a language related to Finnish spoken mainly in the Russian Republic of Karelia. š, č and ǯ are pronounced like the first sounds in shirt, chat and jug; ä is like the a in cat; y is like u in French.
