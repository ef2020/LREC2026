# Puzzle 11 — Lardil

Source: UKLO 2023 — https://www.uklo.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/2023_R1_7-Lardil.zip

## Context

The Lardil language, spoken on Mornington Island in Queensland, Australia by a handful of Lardil people, has a complex but logical system of pronouns. 

Below are the Lardil words for 21 groups of people and their English translations. Note that some Lardil words have multiple English translations.
