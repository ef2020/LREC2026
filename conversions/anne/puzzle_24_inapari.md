# Puzzle 24 — Inapari

Source: UKLO 2020 — https://www.uklo.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/2020_10.-Inapari.pdf

## Context

Inapari is a critically endangered language spoken by 4 individuals in the town of the same name on the border of Peru, Bolivia and Brazil. Here are some Inapari verbs with their English translations.
