My foot, his feet in Zapotec (5 marks) 
Isthmus Zapotec is a language spoken in the state of Oaxaca in the southern part of  
Mexico, west of the Isthmus of Tehuantepec. Based on a 2000 census, it is the native  
language of 75,000-100,000 people.  
  
Examine the following data from this language and complete the tasks. Note: The data are 
presented in phonetic transcription (with symbols of the International Phonetic Alphabet). 
The symbols ʒ and ʃ stand for the consonants in the middle of pleasure and washer, and Ɂ is 
the glottal stop. Note that Zapotec distinguishes singular and plural ‘you’.
